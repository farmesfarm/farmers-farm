Our Story

From Field to
Your Morning Cup

Farmers Farm was born from a simple belief: the best cup of tea starts long before the kettle boils. Our farmers tend to every plant with care, harvesting only at peak potency to bring the truest flavours to your home.

7 TEA AM is our signature morning blend — a precise union of seven carefully selected ingredients that energize, soothe, and nourish from the very first sip of the day.

What's Inside

7 Ingredients.
One Perfect Morning.

🍃
Assam Green Tea
Rich, malty base leaves sourced from the lush valleys of Assam — the backbone of every sip of 7 TEA AM.
🫚
Pure Ginger Root
Freshly dried ginger for a warming kick that awakens digestion and boosts morning metabolism naturally.
🌿
Tulsi (Holy Basil)
India's sacred adaptogen — calms the mind while sharpening focus, setting the perfect tone for your day.
🌼
Chamomile Flowers
Sun-dried chamomile blossoms add a delicate floral sweetness and gentle anti-inflammatory properties.
🍋
Lemon Peel Zest
Bright citrus notes from organic lemon peel that refresh the palate and add a hit of natural Vitamin C.
🌰
Cardamom Pods
Aromatic green cardamom lends a sweet spice that elevates the blend — the signature scent of 7 TEA AM.
🖤
Black Pepper Extract
A trace of black pepper amplifies the absorption of every other ingredient — the invisible seventh secret.
